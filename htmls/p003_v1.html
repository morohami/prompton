~ A Note of Gratitude ~

To my dearest Margaret,

I find myself sitting at the kitchen table this Sunday morning with a cup of tea and a heart so full I scarcely know where to begin. Your handwritten card arrived on Wednesday afternoon — the very day, in fact, when I had run out of small comforts.

The casserole was a kindness beyond words. It arrived warm, wrapped with the kind of care only someone who truly cares would think to provide. We sat as a family that night for the first time in two weeks, and I held my husband's hand while the children ate seconds.

You have a way of showing up that I want my children to grow up understanding. Thank you, dear friend, for being exactly who you are.

With all my love,
Eleanor
