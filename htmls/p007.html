Nonna's Lemon Cake

handed down, not down-shifted
🕐 1h 15m👥 serves 8★ easy

Ingredients

  • 3 lemons, zest + juice
  • 225g unsalted butter, soft
  • 225g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs, room temp
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp warm milk

Method

  1. Heat oven to 180°C. Line a loaf tin.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until pale, about 4 minutes.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, then zest. Fold in flour and milk.
  4. Bake 50 minutes until a skewer comes out clean.
  5. While warm, brush with lemon juice and a spoon of sugar.
